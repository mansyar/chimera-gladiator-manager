# Chimera Gladiator Manager

> Engineer monstrous chimeras from six bio strains and field them in cutthroat automated arena championships.

An auto-battler / management simulation where you run a dark-science monster lab. Stitch together modular creature parts, gamble on genetic instability, and let your creations fight for glory — and gold.

## Features & Gameplay

### Modular Fusion Lab

Build your chimeras from four equipment slots — **HEAD**, **TORSO**, **ARMS**, and **LEGS** — each drawn from six distinct bio strains: Undead, Robotic, Draconic, Beast, Elemental, and Aberrant. Every combination produces unique stats, abilities, and a one-of-a-kind visual appearance. The head you choose even dictates how your chimera behaves in combat.

### Genetic Instability

The defining twist. Stitching together disparate species carries cascading penalties:

- **Purebreds** (all parts the same strain): Reliable, predictable, and immune to berserk and decay — but limited to a single strain's abilities.
- **Hybrids** (2+ strains): Unlock volatile power spikes and cross-strain abilities — but risk berserk (turning on allies), genetic decay (permanent stat damage), and unpredictable combat behavior.

Every assembly is a calculated gamble: raw power versus maintenance cost.

### Hands-Off Tactical Combat

Combat is fully automated — once the match begins, your chimeras fight on their own. Victory is decided by pre-match preparation:

- Scout the enemy's full formation and composition before you position.
- Place your three chimeras on a 3×3 formation grid — front, mid, or back row — to control reach, lanes, and flanking.
- Seven AI behavior modules (determined by the head part) govern targeting, ability priority, and positioning tendencies.
- Matches end when one side is wiped out, or after a 60-second timer — highest HP percentage wins.

### Black Market Economy

Stock your lab on a two-tier black market:

- **Base stock** is always available — reliable common parts to fill gaps or rebuild.
- **Rotating stock** refreshes after every match, offering upgrades and rare finds across four rarity tiers: Common, Uncommon, Rare, and Legendary.
- Legendary parts may demand a minimum Infamy to purchase — the black market only deals premium goods to proven managers.
- Unstable hybrids risk genetic decay, turning the clinic into a recurring gold sink.

### Campaign Progression

A continuous campaign — no roguelike runs, no permadeath. Your lab persists as you climb four escalating tournament tiers, each gated by Infamy and Gold entry fees. Retire legendary champions (after 10 wins) to the Hall of Fame, unlocking permanent research bonuses across three branches: Strain Mastery, Lab Engineering, and Combat Doctrine. Fall behind, and fail-safe mechanics — always-free regular matches, rubber-band difficulty, and emergency salvage — ensure you're never trapped in an unwinnable state.
